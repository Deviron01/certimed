Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about CertMed

How does CertMed verify medicines?

CertMed uses multiple verification methods including QR codes, SMS verification, and manual code entry. Each product is assigned a unique identifier that can be verified against our secure database to confirm authenticity.

Is CertMed free to use?

Yes, CertMed is free for consumers to verify medicines. We partner with pharmaceutical manufacturers who cover the costs of the verification system to ensure public safety.

How can my company join CertMed?

Pharmaceutical manufacturers can register through our manufacturer portal. The process involves company verification, system integration, and training. Contact our partnerships team for more information.

What happens if a medicine is counterfeit?

If a verification attempt indicates a counterfeit product, we immediately alert the user and provide guidance on reporting the suspicious product to appropriate authorities. We also notify the manufacturer and regulatory bodies.

How secure is the verification data?

We use industry-standard encryption and blockchain technology to secure all verification data. Personal information is protected according to strict privacy policies and we comply with all relevant data protection regulations.
