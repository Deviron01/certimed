About CertMed

Leading the fight against counterfeit medicines through innovative technology and collaboration

Our Mission

At CertMed, we are dedicated to protecting public health by combating the global threat of counterfeit medicines. Our mission is to create a world where every medicine can be instantly verified as authentic, ensuring patient safety and maintaining trust in the pharmaceutical supply chain.

We leverage cutting-edge technology including QR codes, blockchain, and artificial intelligence to provide a comprehensive solution for medicine authentication that is accessible to everyone, from healthcare professionals to individual consumers.

Through partnerships with pharmaceutical manufacturers, regulatory bodies, and healthcare providers, we are building a global network that makes counterfeit medicines a thing of the past.

Protecting Lives Through Technology

Every verification we perform helps protect someone from potentially harmful counterfeit medicines.
